**pona cards** is a simple card game where the aim is to collect as many cards as possible. The game provides 397 cards, all of which are based on words
from the constructed language Toki Pona. Each card has a rarity and meaning.

**_NOT RECOMMENDED FOR LEARNERS!_** Most cards in the game represent words that are archaic or unused! Do not use this game to learn Toki Pona,
it won't help.

How to play?
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Upon starting the game, you'll be met with a pack of cards and two navigation buttons at the top of the screen.
Click on the pack to open it and unlock 3 cards. You can open 5 packs per day.
The navigation buttons allow you to move between sections on the website. The "Collection" button will take you to a list of all cards you own, while the "Pack" button will bring you back to the menu where you can open a pack.
